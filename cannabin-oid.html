What is Cannabin-Oid?

Cannabin-Oid is a free AI-powered information platform for cannabis users, patients, and curious people. It helps you identify strains, understand terpene profiles, navigate medical prescription pathways, and connect with a harm-reduction community.

It's part of the FeelFamous ecosystem — built by a cannabis patient, for cannabis patients, on free tools, with no data harvesting.

If you're at Spannabis

Welcome to Bilbao. Welcome to the future of cannabis in Europe. This is the first Spannabis at the Bilbao Exhibition Centre — a historic moment.

The Basque Country has the most stable Cannabis Social Club (CSC) environment in Spain right now. While Barcelona has been shutting clubs, the Basque Country has been building community.

CSC Culture Here
Cannabis Social Clubs are private associations — not shops, not coffee shops. You need an existing member to sponsor you, valid ID, and a 24–48 hour wait. Annual membership is typically €15–50. Cash only. Consume on premises only.
Public Consumption
Public consumption can result in fines of €300–600. Keep it in the club. Be discreet. Respect the community that keeps these spaces legal.
⚠️ Know Before You Go
Cannabis laws vary significantly by country and region. This information is for harm reduction purposes only. Always research the specific laws where you are. If you're travelling internationally, know your country's import/export laws — they are serious.

UK Medical Cannabis

Since Royal Decree 903/2025, Spain has legalised medical cannabis through hospital dispensing. The UK has had medical cannabis available on prescription since 2018 — but access has been slow and expensive through private clinics.

If you're self-medicating and want to go legal, Cannabin-Oid can help you understand the pathway, the clinics, and what to expect.
