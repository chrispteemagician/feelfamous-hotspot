What is FeelFamous?

FeelFamous is a network of community villages — each one built around a niche, powered by AI, hosted for free, and owned by nobody. No algorithm. No ads. No data harvesting.

Each village has an AI expert character who speaks the community’s language, a directory of members with their own pages, and one rule: core features free forever.

The model is Coffeeware — free to use, buy the builder a coffee if you want to, never required. The service never degrades if you can’t pay.

Your Hamlet

Every person in the ecosystem gets a hamlet — their own page with their own URL. Not a profile in someone’s database. A real page. Permanent until you say otherwise.

60 seconds to create. No account. No password. Just a name, a craft, a location. A place to be found.
